From the North (working title) is a historical novel set in the province of Judea during the Great Jewish Revolt (66-73AD) against the Roman Empire. It follows the fortunes of two men. The first is John Levi. A deserter from the Roman VI legion, he comes back home seeking peace but finds himself swept up by a war he does everything in his power to avoid. The second is Eleazar. A son of a former High Priest of the Jewish Temple, he is a member of the elite of Jerusalem and Judea. When rebellion breaks out he is forced to navigate the treacherous landscape of Jerusalem’s politics.
